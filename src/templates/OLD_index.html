Core Features

Everything You Need for Smart Meal Prep

Experience the future of meal preparation with our comprehensive suite of AI-powered tools designed to streamline your kitchen workflow.

AI-Powered Meal Planning

Generate personalized weekly meal plans using Google Gemini AI based on your pantry items, time constraints, and dietary preferences. Never run out of meal ideas again.

Batch Prep Scheduling

Optimize your cooking time with intelligent batch prep schedules. Group meals into efficient cooking sessions that maximize your productivity in the kitchen.

Smart Pantry Tracking

Keep track of your pantry inventory with AI-powered expiration date predictions. Reduce food waste and always know what ingredients you have available.

Intelligent Shopping Lists

Automatically generate shopping lists from your meal plans. Track costs, set budgets, and make smarter purchasing decisions with real-time budget monitoring.

Meal Planning Calendar

Organize your meals with an intuitive calendar view. Schedule prep sessions, track what you've cooked, and plan ahead for busy weeks.

Budget & Analytics

Monitor your spending with detailed cost tracking per meal and overall savings analysis. Make data-driven decisions about your food budget.
